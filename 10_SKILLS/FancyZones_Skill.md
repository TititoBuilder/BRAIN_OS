---

## How to Open Layout Editor

| Method | Steps |
|---|---|
| **Drag shortcut** | Hold `Win+Shift` while dragging a window — FancyZones editor opens |
| **PowerToys UI** | PowerToys → FancyZones → **Launch Layout Editor** button |

---

## How to Snap a Window to a Zone

1. Hold `Shift` while dragging the window
2. Zones highlight as you hover over them
3. Release to snap into zone
